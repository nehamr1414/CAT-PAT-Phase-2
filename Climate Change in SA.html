Climate Change in South Africa

It is important to understand how climate change is affecting South Africa so we can successfully address its impacts and work towards a more sustainable future.

A few of the ways South Africa has been affected by climate change are:

  • Increased temperatures
  • Water scarcity
  • Extreme weather events
  • Impact on biodiversity
  • Health risks
