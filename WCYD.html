How YOU Can Help the Climate Crisis

Actions You Can Take at Home

  • Reduce energy use by switching off lights and unplugging devices when not in use.
  • Recycle and compost to minimize waste sent to landfills.
  • Opt for energy-efficient appliances and LED bulbs.
  • Conserve water by fixing leaks and using water-saving fixtures.
  • Switch to solar water heaters.

Making a Difference in Your Community

  • Use public transport, cycle, or walk instead of driving alone.
  • Support local and sustainable businesses.
  • Participate in community clean-ups and tree planting events.
  • Advocate for climate-friendly policies and educate others.
  • Volunteer with environmental organizations.
