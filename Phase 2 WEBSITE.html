The Effects of Climate Change on South Africa

Climate change is a rising issue faced in South Africa, and across the globe, today. It affects cities, ecosystems, and communities in our country. Rising temperatures, water shortages, and extreme weather disasters are a few of the problems caused by climate change and are becoming more and more common. In this website I will focus on how the major cities in South Africa - Johannesburg, Cape Town, and Durban - are affected and how they adapt through methods such as renewable energy, waste management, and resilience planning.
